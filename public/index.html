Submit the following items to social media for review using the hashtag #COVIDARTPROJECT, along with the project number.

  1. Take a video of yourself reading the ingredients of your sanitizing products - out-loud. Extra points for dramatic readings.
  2. Take a screen grab of your last zoom or video chat session, name the characters and submit a short dialogue to go with it.
  3. Gather your entire toilet paper roll supply, work out how many meters that is, create a sign that says “I have XXX meters of toilet paper, enough to XXX” and photograph the sign and the toilet paper together.
  4. Photograph yourself wearing whatever you are wearing to cover your face. If nothing, a photo of how you cough in public.
  5. Take a photo of you and all the people and pets you are living with wearing pieces of each other’s clothes.
  6. Draw a picture of the thing you miss the most about going outside.
  7. Find a photo of yourself doing something you can’t or wouldn’t do in the age of COVID and describe what is happening in the photo the way you would a historic photograph.
  8. Submit the recipe that’s made you the happiest post-COVID.
  9. Submit a photo of your work-from-home setup with a detailed description.
  10. Take a photo of you in your “work” clothes. Describe their functionality.
  11. Create a song or dance about getting groceries and/or homeschooling.
  12. Create a flip book or comic of a quarantine exercise routine.
  13. Create a motivational banner, lawn sign or chalk your driveway somewhere for the neighbors to see.
  14. Paint something on your roof that will be photographed by satellite for google earth.
